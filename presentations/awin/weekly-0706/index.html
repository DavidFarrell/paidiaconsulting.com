Metrics

  • Avoiding performative metrics (number goes up without business value)
  • Focusing on meaningful metrics (only move when real value goes up)
  • Thinking through leading and lagging indicators
  • Calls fed in · leading, performative - volume through the pipe; can be pumped without any value landing
  • Briefings generated · leading, slightly performative - output count; says nothing about use
  • Voluntary repeat use · leading, meaningful - an AM comes back to the briefing unprompted; hard to fake
  • Briefing rated useful · leading, slightly meaningful - one-tap rating; qualitative (junior vs senior "why"?)
  • Prep time saved / retention · lagging, meaningful - the true outcome, but reads late
  • Capture pipeline running · a gate - binary, on or off; a precondition rather than a score
