How transcripts are handled

  1. Kept inside Awin's secure Microsoft 365 environment - the same place our meeting recordings and files already live.
  2. Access limited to the Samsung account team and the small Awin AI team building the system.
  3. Used only to serve the Samsung account - never shared with, or used for, any other client.
A1

If it helps: an agreed redaction pass

  1. Together we agree the kinds of content that should never be kept - personal anecdotes, candid asides, anything off-record.
  2. Those are removed automatically before a transcript is stored.
  3. Samsung can see what's held, on request.
A2

If it helps: a fixed retention period

  1. Transcripts are kept only for the development phase.
  2. At the end of it they're deleted, and we agree together what - if anything - is kept longer.
  3. By then we'll all know much more about what's useful: we decide with evidence, not up front.
A3

If needed: distil and discard

  1. We keep only the extracted facts - commitments, decisions, factual updates - and delete the transcript.
  2. What's kept looks like: “new campaign lead starts in September” · “agreed to revisit the publisher mix in Q4”.
  3. The honest trade-off: the AI can never re-read the originals, so improvements can't reach past history.
