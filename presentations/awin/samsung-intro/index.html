What Samsung gets, and what we're asking

What you get

  • Continuity through change. As the team around the account evolves, nothing gets lost - new people arrive already knowing the history.
  • No more re-explaining. Every call starts from full context; questions you've answered stay answered.
  • More strategy, less admin. Time goes on the why behind the numbers, not on gathering data from scattered sources.
  • First in line. Samsung shapes what gets built, and benefits from it first.

What we're asking

  • Your OK to transcribe our meetings together (Teams transcription on).
  • David, the AI specialist building this, joins some meetings - for example the daily stand-up - to learn how the account runs.
  • That's it. No new process, no extra work on your side.

How transcripts are handled

  1. Kept inside Awin's secure Microsoft 365 environment - the same place our meeting recordings and files already live.
  2. Access limited to the Samsung account team. Used only to serve this account - nothing else.
  3. A redaction pass removes personal and off-record content before anything is kept.
• If it helps, we can also agree a fixed retention period (transcripts deleted at the end of the pilot, then we decide together what happens next) and the categories of content that are never kept, with sight of what's held on request.
